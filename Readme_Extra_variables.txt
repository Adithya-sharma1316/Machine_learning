ITC.csv is the dataset which contains stock market prices of ITC company

Special variables used :

 1. mov_avg5 - This is the average closing price of the stock over the last 5 trading days.
 2. mov_avg10 - This is the average closing price of the stock over the last 10 trading days.
 3. close - This is the closing price of the stock one day prior.
 4. close1 - This is the closing price of the stock two days prior.
